VAG Connect

Control your Audi, Volkswagen or Škoda from Homey.

How to add your car

1 Activate connected services in the manufacturer app

Your car must be set up in your brand's official app before Homey can reach it:

  • Audi → download myAudi, connect your car, verify you see live data
  • Volkswagen → download We Connect, activate connected services
  • Škoda → download MyŠkoda, activate Škoda Connect

If the official app can't show your car's status, this app won't work either.

2 Add the device in Homey
  • Tap Devices → + → VAG Connect
  • Choose your brand: Audi Vehicle, Volkswagen Vehicle or Škoda Vehicle
  • Enter the email & password you use in the official app
  • Your vehicles will appear — tap the one you want and tap Add
3 Optional: tweak per-vehicle settings

Tap your car in Homey → Settings:

SettingWhat it does
S-PIN4-digit Security PIN required by some models to lock/unlock. Leave blank if not needed.
Poll intervalHow often Homey fetches live data (default 5 min, minimum 2 min).
⚠️ Remote commands (lock, climate start) can take 5–30 seconds to reach the car — this is normal and depends on the car's cellular connection.

Supported features

  • 🔒 Lock / Unlock doors
  • 🌡️ Start / Stop cabin pre-conditioning with target temperature
  • 🪟 Window heating toggle
  • 🔋 Battery level & low-battery alarm
  • 📏 Estimated driving range (km)
  • ⚡ Charging status
  • 📲 Full Homey Flow support
